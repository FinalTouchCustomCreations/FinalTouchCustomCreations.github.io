Free Estimates

FTCC and FTHMS work together to offer our clients FREE estimates on theri projects. Our process is a bit different that what you may have experinace in the past. The first step is to meet with you where we will get all your ideas, measurements, and what you want to accomplish with your remodel. From there FTCC will begin creating your 3D rendering to bring your ideas to life. Once you approve and have all your materials picked out we will being building your estimat based on your choices.
